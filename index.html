About

SAAS
ClarifEye is a SAAS (Software as a Service Platform) for companies and SMEs that enables them to hire the best candidates by using a state of the art recruitment system.
NETWORK
By creating a network of employers and employees, the system encourages a rating system to thrive which in turn allows the best candidate to be selected by companies.
DASHBOARD
By offering a unique and customizable dashboard for recruiters to choose their optimal candidates, ClarifEye offers the ability for such recruiters to hire the best candidates the field has to offer.
EMPEE SCORE
ClarifEye offers its own scoring system - EMPEE that scores the individuals on the network based on the request of the recruiters. This helps the recruiters choose the most suitable candidates for any job description.

How it works?

01
RECRUITER

Wants to find suitable candidates for a job.

Will login into his portal on our dashboard and post the job along with requirements.

02
UNDER THE HOOD

Our ML models recommend the best candidates on our network with an EMPEE score.

Taking the skills required as parameters for processing logic, our ML models devise an EMPEE score for the optimal candidates and present the list to the recruiter in real time.

03
RECRUITER

Checks the background of the top candidates he/she finds suitable and wants to interview.

A reqeust for an interview is also facilitated by our network.

04
IMMEDIATE BACKGROUND CHECK

ClarifEye enables the selected candidates' background check to be done within seconds.

Since both the employees and employers enter the network after rigourous verification, a candidate's previous companies are auto-verified when he/she applies for a new job.
